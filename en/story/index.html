Our Story

From the sunset to Mars

Inspired by the red glow of the West Sea sunset, the name carries a double meaning — the red planet Mars and Hwaseong (華城) in Gyeonggi.

The brewery opened in April 2022; the first product took a year and three months. The hardest challenge: achieving grape color and flavor without sweeteners or dyes, within Korea's takju regulations.

"Until Elon Musk carries Mars to Mars."
